Who are we?

Harvest British College provides English Language training Courses with it's specializations, advisory and Educational services. Over the Course of 12 years, the Company has grown to include 10 Branches in multiple locations as Greater Cairo, and Alexandria. Harvest is currently empowering 120,000 Students of all age groups to Excel in the English Language.
Our Academic advisors and Institutions have helped us successfully cross the cultural barrier.
As an Educational Corporation, Harvest is accredited by the British Cambridge Institution for Training, and we are proud to have more than 150,000 Graduates with an accredited Cambridge College Certificate. More recently, we have additionally acquired accreditation within the Training Centers of the Central Agency for Organization and Administration.

Our Vision

Harvest College is the perfect Educational Institution for the ambitious and driven Individual. We offer a wide range of Courses in Language Teaching, Cultural Studies, and more!
